About Us

Dasmen Residential is a private real estate investment company that purchases and operates multi family properties in major cities throughout the United States. Our intention is to sustain cash-flow, while attentively preserving the value of our properties.Our goal is to to maintain a long-term investment approach, by carefully conditioning, servicing, and often upgrading the properties. We have considerable satisfaction in our track record as a respected property operator and manager, and we guarantee to continue in that direction by improving the quality of life for our residents.
